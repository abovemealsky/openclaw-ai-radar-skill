# AI Radar

AI Radar generates concise AI intelligence briefs focused on artificial intelligence developments. It tracks:
- model launches
- research breakthroughs
- important open-source AI tools
- major industry signals

The goal is to highlight the most important developments and explain why they matter.

## When to use this skill

Use this skill when the user wants:
- a daily AI news brief
- a weekly AI industry summary
- a summary of important AI research
- trending AI open-source projects
- a quick overview of AI industry trends

Example requests:
- Generate today's AI Radar.
- Summarize this week's AI developments.
- What are the most important AI research trends this week?

## Output style

The AI Radar brief should prioritize:
- high signal
- concise summaries
- clear explanations

For each item include:
- title
- short summary
- why it matters
